Flordespine invited Bradament.

XL

"My sister the request could ill deny; And so they came together to the place, Where, but for you, by that ill squadron I Had been compelled the cruel flame to face: There Flordespina made her family Caress and do my sister no small grace; And, having in a female robe arraid, Past her on all beholders for a maid.

XLI

"Because perceiving vantage there was none In the male cheer by which she was misled, The damsel held it wise, reproach to shun, Which might by any carping tongue be said. And this the rather: that the ill, which one Of the two garments in her mind had bred, Now with the other which revealed the cheat, She would assay to drive from her conceit.
