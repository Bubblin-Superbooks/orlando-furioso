Thus each bestirs himself like valorous knight, Who follows Clermont's banner to the fight.

LVI

Seven hundred men with good Rinaldo speed, Drawn from Mount Alban and the townships nigh — No fiercer erst obeyed Achilles' lead — Enured to summer and to winter sky: So stout each warrior is, so good at need, A hundred would not from a thousand fly; And, better than some famous cavaliers, Many amid that squadron couch their spears.
