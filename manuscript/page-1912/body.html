LXXVII

Not in Biserta's port his host to land Was the sage king of Africa's intent, Who had sure news that shore by Nubia's band Was held, but he so far above it meant To steer his Moorish squadron, that the strand Should not be steep or rugged for descent: There would he disembark, and thence would aid Forthwith his people, broken and dismaid.
