Whether Chance moved Mountalban's martial lord, Or Malagigi, provident and sage, That knew how young Rogero's charmed sword Cleft helm and hauberk in its greedy rage, One and the other warrior made accord, (As said without their faulchions to engage. )

The place of combat chosen by that twain Was near old Arles, upon a spacious plain.
