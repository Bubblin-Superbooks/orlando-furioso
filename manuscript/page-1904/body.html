LX

Then said, as erst Silenus said — when seen, And taken sleeping the cave of yore — SOLVITE ME, with visage so serene, With look so much less wayward than before, That him they from his bonds delivered clean, And raiment to the naked warrior bore; All comforting their friend, with grief opprest For that delusion which had him possest.
