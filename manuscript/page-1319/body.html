XLIX

In that, which looks towards the western sun, Is lodged the giant monarch of Argier; And him assist his serpent-hide to don Bold Ferrau and Circassia's cavalier. Gradasso and the puissant Falsiron, In that which fronts the morning hemisphere, Clothe with their hands, in Trojan plate and chain, The good successor of King Agricane.

L

High on a throne of ample state appeared Agramant and Marsilius; next in place Were Stordilane and all the chiefs, revered Throughout the squadrons of the paynim race. Happy was he who found himself upreared On mound or tree, above that level space. Great was the throng, and round the palisade On every side the eddying people swayed.
