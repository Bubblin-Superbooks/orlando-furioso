Arms are the trade of some, by some are scanned Letters and arts; another tills the plain: One serves in court, by other guided go The herd as pleases her who rules below.

XIII

"A boy departed with they youthful peers, Who was of cruel Clytemnestra born; Like lily fresh (he numbered eighteen years) Or blooming rose, new-gathered from the thorn. He having armed a bark, his pinnace steers In search of plunder, o'er the billows borne. With him a hundred other youths engage, Picked from all Greece, and of their leader's age.
