"Homer a conqueror Agamemnon shows, And makes the Trojan seem of coward vein, And from the suitors, faithful to her vows, Penelope a thousand wrongs sustain: Yet — would'st thou I the secret should expose? — By contraries throughout the tale explain: That from the Trojan bands the Grecian ran; And deem Penelope a courtezan.

XXVIII

"What fame Eliza, she so chaste of sprite, On the other hand, has left behind her, hear! Who widely is a wanton baggage hight, Solely that she to Maro was not dear, Marvel not this should cause me sore despite, And if my speech diffusive should appear. Authors I love, and pay the debt I owe, Speaking their praise; an author I below!
