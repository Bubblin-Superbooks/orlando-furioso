LIII

"If there be gain, ye know, Late thirty-two, Your vassal kings, with you our sails we spread; Now, if we pause to sum the account anew, Hardly a third survives; the rest are dead. May it please Heaven no further loss ensue! But if you will pursue your quest, I dread Lest not a fourth nor fifth will soon remain; And wholly spent will be your wretched train.
