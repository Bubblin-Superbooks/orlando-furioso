XXXIX

This was fair Flordelice, whose bosom so Burned with the love of Monodantes' son, She, when she left him prisoner to his foe At that streight bridge, had nigh distracted gone. From France had she past hither — given to know — By that proud paynim, who the deed had done, How Brandimart, with many cavaliers, Was prisoner in the city of Algiers.
