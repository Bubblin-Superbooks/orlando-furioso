LXII

"Such our Rogero is, ye know and I, That — pitted one to one in listed fight — Not Roland, not Rinaldo stands more high, Nor whatsoever other Christian knight. But would ye kindle warfare far and nigh, Though superhuman be that champion's might, The warrior is but one mid many spears, Matched singly with a host of martial peers.
