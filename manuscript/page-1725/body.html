LXI

"Say this and nought beside, and would he hear My name, declare that 'tis to thee unknown." With wonted kindness cried that dame, "I ne'er In spending life itself, not words alone, Should weary in your service; since whilere You would in my behalf as much have done." Her Aymon's daughter thanked in courteous strain, And to her hand consigned Frontino's rein.
