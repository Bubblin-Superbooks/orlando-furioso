CLXXIII

"Oh! how thy foes will by the death of thee Be freed henceforward from alarm and fear! Alas! how strengthened paynimry will be! What hardiment will now be theirs! what cheer! What of thy consort will become? I see Even here her mourning, and her outcries hear. Me she accuses, haply hates, I know; In that, through me, her every hope lies low.
