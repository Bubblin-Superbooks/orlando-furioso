To her returning yet again; the dame To him who showed to her such courteous lore, Refused not to disclose her martial name, Since he agreed to tell the style be bore. She quickly satisfied the warrior's claim; To learn his title she desired so sore. "I am Marphisa," the virago cried: All else was known, as bruited far and wide.
