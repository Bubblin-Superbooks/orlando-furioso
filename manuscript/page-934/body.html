XXXVIII

"Yet Orontea lived, while of that shore The other settlers all were dead and gone; And now ten times as many such or more Had into strength and greater credit grown. Nor for ten forges, often closed, in store Have the ill-furnished band more files than one; And the ten champions have as well the care To welcome shrewdly all who thither fare.
