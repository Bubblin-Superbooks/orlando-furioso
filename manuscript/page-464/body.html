LXXXI

The remnant of the winter, he with shield And spear achieved things worthy to be shown, I ween; but these were then so well concealed, It is no fault of mine they are not blown; For good Orlando was in fighting field, Prompter to do, than make his prowess known. Nor e'er was bruited action of the knight, Save when some faithful witness was in sight.

LXXXII
