CXXX

"For remedy, he sends in haste a band Of messengers, with letters far and nigh. Some of Argia here, some there demand; Nor town unsearched is left in Lombardy. Next he in person goes; nor any land Leaves unexamined by himself or spy. Yet cannot he discover means or way For learning where concealed his consort lay.

CXXXI
