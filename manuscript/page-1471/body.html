LXV

Not unavenged the unhappy monarch dies; For in the very moment he is smit, The sword — for little period his — he plies, And good Rogero's vizor would have split. But that he stopt the stroke in wary wise, And broke its force and vigour ere it lit; Its force and vigour broke: for he, below The better arm, first smote his Tartar foe.
