LXXXI

King Charles and peers him sheathed in plate and shell Deem not Rogero, but the emperor's son; And viewing in that combat fierce and fell Such force and quickness by the stripling shown; And, without e'er offending her, how well That knight defends himself, now change their tone; Esteem both well assorted; and declare The champions worthy of each other are.
