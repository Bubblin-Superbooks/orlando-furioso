LVIII

Orlando to the rabble cried, "Untie The cavalier, unless you would be slain." — "Who deals such mighty blows?" — one made reply, That would be thought the truest of the train; "Were he of fire who makes such bold defy, We wax or straw, too haughty were the strain": And charged with that the paladin of France. Orlando at the losel couched his lance.
