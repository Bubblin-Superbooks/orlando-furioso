LXXVII

"He leaves his native land with this intent, Nor letteth any his departure know; And coasts, in tears and making sad lament, The marshes that about his city go: He his heart's queen, amid his discontent, Meanwhile forgets not, for this second woe. Lo! him another accident that falls, From sovereign woe to sovereign bliss recalls!
