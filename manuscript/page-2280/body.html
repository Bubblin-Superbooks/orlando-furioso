LVII

"Since here Rogero is not, to contest The bride's possession with the stranger knight, Lest he, as undefended, be opprest, And forfeit so without dispute his right, On his behalf I undertake this quest, — His sister I — against whatever wight Shall here assert a claim to Bradamant, Or more desert than good Rogero vaunt."

LVIII
