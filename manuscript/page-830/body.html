CXXIII

And why they had those arms, without the fear Of Syria's king, pulled down, and to his slight. Of his champions England's cavalier, Sir Gryphon courteously informed aright. But little of those arms, pursued the peer, He knew, which were the occasion of the fight; But (for he thither with Marphisa came And Sansonnet had armed to aid the dame. )
