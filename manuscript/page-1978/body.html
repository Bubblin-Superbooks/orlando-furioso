XLIII

"And how to think, from love those counsels flow Which once you bore and bear me, as you say, (To speak the very truth I do not know, )

Who with Orlando see you here, this day. I ween that, knowing you are doomed to woe, And marked for the devouring dragon's prey, Ye all mankind would drag to nether hell, In your eternity of pains to dwell.
