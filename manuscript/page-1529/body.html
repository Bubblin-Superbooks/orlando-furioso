XCII

Hence had that king repaired to the sea-shore, The place assigned to end their discord fell: But all was marred by Malagigi's lore; Who, cheating good Rinaldo with a spell, To sea the champion in a pinnace bore. Too tedious were the tale at length to tell. Hence evermore Gradasso had opined, The gentle baron was of craven kind.
