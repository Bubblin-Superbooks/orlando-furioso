XIX

The Christian host throughout is overthrown, And how they know not, in tumultuous wise; And that it is a wonted insult done By Switzer or by Gascon, some surmise; But — since the reason is to most unknown — Each several nation to its standard flies, This to the drum, that to the trumpet's sound, And shriek and shout from earth to heaven redound.
