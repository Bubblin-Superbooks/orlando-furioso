XXXVI

"To that foul murderous shore by chance did fare, After long years elapsed, a youthful wight, Whose fathers sprung from good Alcides were, And he, of proof in arms, Elbanio hight; There was he seized, of peril scarce aware, As unsuspecting such a foul despite: And, closely guarded, into prison flung, Kept for like cruel use the rest among.
