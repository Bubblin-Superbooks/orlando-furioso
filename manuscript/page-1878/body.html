IV

Meanwhile the weird Melissa, she — the font Of all that wizards or enchanters know — Had by her art transformed her female front, And taken Argier's mighty shape; in show And gesture she appeared as Rodomont, And seemed, like him, in dragon's hide to go: Such was her belied sword and such her shield; Nor aught was wanting which he wore afield.

V
