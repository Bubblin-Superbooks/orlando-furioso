XXXII

What he rehearsed, the Gascon knight believed, Nor without cause believed the news he bore, A rumour universally received And bruited through the squadrons of the Moor; Who had that notion of their love conceived From signs of kindness witnessed evermore. For — good or bad — though from one mouth it flows, Fame to a boundless torrent quickly grows.
