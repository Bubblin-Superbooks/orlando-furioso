LXXVII

Now stopt, now circled, now retired the knight, And oft his hand his foot accompanied; And lifted shield, and shifted sword in fight, Where shifting he the hostile hand espied. Either he smote her not, or — die he smite — Smote, where he deemed least evil would betide. The lady, ere the westering sun descend, Desires to bring that duel to an end.
