XXIII

Successively that castellain displayed One hight of Marca, of the Anjouites three. How "Marsi, Daunians, Salentines," (he said) "And Bruci, these shall oft molest, you see: Yet not by Frank or Latian's friendly aid Shall one delivered from destruction be. Lo! from the realm, as oft as they attack, Alphonso and Gonsalvo beat them back.
