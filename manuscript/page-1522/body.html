LXXVII

She parts, and has anew already planned Thither with good Rinaldo to resort; With Guido, Sansonet of doughty hand, Or other cavalier of Pepin's court; Some warrior good by water and by land, That with the Saracen will well assort. Who, if no stronger than her baffled knight, With better fortune may maintain the fight.
