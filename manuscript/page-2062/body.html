"Wherefore ten years ago wast thou not known, So that I counselled might have been of thee? Before the sorrows and the grief begun, That have nigh quenched my eyes; but raised shall be The curtain from the scene, that thou upon My pain mayst look, and mayst lament with me; And I to thee of mine unheard-of woe The argument and very head will show.

XI

"Above, was left a neighbouring city, pent Within a limpid stream that forms a lake; Which widens, and wherein Po finds a vent. Their way the waters from Benacus take. Built was the city, when to ruin went Walls founded by the Agenorean snake. Here me of gentle line my mother bore, But of small means, in humble home and poor.
