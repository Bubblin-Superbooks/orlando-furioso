Sarza's monarch more Loved than his realm, — beyond his very eyes: And valour showed for her and courteous lore, Not knowing yet she was another's prize. If he had, — then, — then, first, — the story known, Even what he did that day, he would have done.

CXVI

At once the foes a thousand ladders rear. Against the wall by the assailants shored, Two mannered each round; the second, in the rear, Urged on by the first; the third the second gored. One mounts the wall through valour, one through fear, And all attempt perforce the dangerous ford; For cruel Rodomont of Argier slays Or smites the wretched laggard who delays.
