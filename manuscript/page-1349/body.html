And would have quickly joined him that was gone, But for the chance of an adventure rare; Which him detained until the day was done, And made him lose the track of Ulien's heir: A woman who had fallen into the Saone, And who without his help had perished there, The warrior drowning in that water found, And stemmed the stream and dragged the dame aground.
