LXIV

To better speed fair Bradamant aroused Her courser, yet but little way did ride, When with his flock, which on the champaign browsed, Leaving the fields, a shepherd she espied. To him where, well or ill, she might be housed, — With many instances the maid applied — For never house could such ill shelter yield, But that in rain 'twere worse to lodge afield.
