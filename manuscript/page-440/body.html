It was the will of Heaven that he, before The King of Ireland, should not reach the land, The he with greater ease upon that shore Might act what shortly you shall understand. "Make for the isle. Now" (said he "may'st thou moor," )

(Thus issuing to the pilot his command), "And give me for my need the skiff; for I Will to the rock without more company.
