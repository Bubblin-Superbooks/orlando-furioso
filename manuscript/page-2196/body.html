A thousand miles and more for this alone He thither measured, and for nought beside. He saith; and from the multitude is gone, And by a road that's shown to him doth ride. For towards the bridge is royal Leo flown; Haply lest him from this the foe divide: Behind him pricks Rogero with such fire, The warrior calls not, nor awaits, his squire.
