XXVI

"So said the knight; and I, who was distraught, And all beside myself, was not aware That the design, in which he help besought, Was manifestly but too foul a snare; And in Geneura's clothes disguised, as taught, Let down (so oft I used the corded stair. )

Nor I the traitor's foul deceit perceived, Until the deadly mischief was achieved.
