XLV

" And yet will I endeavour to obtain For thee, before thou perish, this content; Though much, I fear, 'twill but augment thy pain. And thee protracted death but more torment.'So I the ten encounter,' (said again Elbanio), `I at heart, am confident Myself to save, and enemies to slay; Though made of iron were the whole array.'
