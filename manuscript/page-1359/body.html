Except mine host, those others were agreed That chaste and good their consorts they believed. — "Think each man as he will, but well I read," (The landlord said, "You fondly are deceived: )

Your rash replies to one conclusion lead, That you are all of common sense bereaved; And so too must believe this noble knight, Unless he would persuade us black is white.

CXXXVI

"Because, as single is that precious bird The phoenix, and on earth there is but one, So, in this ample world, it is averred, One only can a woman's treason shun. Each hopes alike to be that wight preferred, The victor who that single palm has won. — How is it possible that what can fall To one alone, should be the lot of all?
