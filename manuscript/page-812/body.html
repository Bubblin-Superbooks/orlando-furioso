LXXXV

Aquilant had in Antioch chanced to know She was his concubine, — well certified Of this by many, — and in furious glow Exclaimed; "Thou falsest robber, thou hast lied!" And dealt, with that, the recreant such a blow, He drove two grinders down his throat; then tied (Not sought Martano with his foe to cope) The caitiff's arms behind him with a rope.
