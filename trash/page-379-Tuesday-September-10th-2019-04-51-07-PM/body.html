XV

Almighty God! how fallible and vain Is human judgment, dimmed by clouds obscure! Bireno's actions, impious and profane, By others are reputed just and pure. Already stooping to their oars, the train Have loosed his vessel from the port secure, And with the duke and his companions steer For Zealand through the deep, with meery cheer.

XVI

Already Holland and its headlands all Are left astern, and now descried no more; Since to shun Friesland they to larboard hawl.
