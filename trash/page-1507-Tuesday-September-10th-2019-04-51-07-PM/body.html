XLV

"Few days are past, since I in shameful wise Saw Roland, running naked in his mood, Sending forth piteous shrieks and fearful cries. In fine, that he is frantic I conclude; Nor this had I believed, save with these eyes That strange and cruel wonder I had viewed." She added next, how from the bridge's top, Embraced by Rodomont, she saw him drop.
