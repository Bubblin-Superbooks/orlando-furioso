XXX

Ulany, that in Bradamant descried, — Known both by voice and ensignry — the maid, Who some few days before those knights of pride With her victorious lance on earth had laid, How, in a town not far remote — replied — An evil race, by pity never swayed, Besides that they their raiment thus had shorn, Had beat them, and had done them other scorn.
