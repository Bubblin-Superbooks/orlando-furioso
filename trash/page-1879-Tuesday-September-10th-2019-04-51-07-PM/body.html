VI

"Let not this battle be pursued, my lord, In that 'twould cost our Moorish cause too dear. Let sin of broken faith and forfeit word Fall upon Rodomont! take thou no fear! Let each now show the metal of his sword, Each for a hundred stands when I am here." So upon Agramant this counsel wrought, That king pressed forward without further thought.

VII

He, thinking that the monarch of Algiers Is with him, of the pact has little care; And would not rate a thousand cavaliers So high, if handed in his aid they were.
