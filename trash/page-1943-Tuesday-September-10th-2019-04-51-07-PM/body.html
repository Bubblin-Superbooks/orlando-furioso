LIII

"So not left out, I care not, if I be The first or last (said Agramant): I know In arms no better can I find than thee, Though I should seek a comrade, high or low, And what (Sobrino cried becomes of me? )

I should be more expert if old in show; And evermore in peril it is good, Force should have Counsel in his neighbourhood."

LIV

Stricken in years, yet vigorous was the sage, And well had proved himself with sword and spear; And said, he found himself in gray old age, Such as in green and supple youth whilere.
