XXXVIII

"You see," (pursued that warder, "how with dead )

Covered is Ghiaradada's green champaign. It seems each city opes her gates through dread; And Venice scarce her freedom can maintain. You see he suffers not the Church's head, Passing the narrow confines of Romagne, Modena from Ferrara's duke to reave; Who would not to that prince a remnant leave.
