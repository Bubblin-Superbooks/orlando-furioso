Whom by such long and by such tedious way She sought throughout the realm of France in vain; Here by the cognizance, his old display, Afar, by her distinguished from the train. At the first sight of her he quits the fray, And wears a semblance loving and humane. He clipt her round with many a fond caress, And kissed a thousand times, or little less.
