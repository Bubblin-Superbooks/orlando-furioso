Who will pursue the brindled beast for ten, Or twenty yards, and, after, stop to bay; When he beholds his flashing eyes, and when He sees the griesly beast his teeth display. 'Twas thus, before those valiant gentlemen And princes, present there in fair array, Fearful Martano, seized with panic dread, Turned to the right his courser's rein and head.
