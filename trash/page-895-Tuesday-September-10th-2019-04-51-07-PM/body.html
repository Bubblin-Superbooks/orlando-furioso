LXVI

A dame, as the Cumean sybil gray, Or Hector's ancient mother of renown, Made call the patron out, and bade him say, If they their lives were willing to lay down; Or were content beneath the yoke to stay, According to the custom of the town, — One of two evils they must choose, — be slain, Or captives, one and all, must there remain.
