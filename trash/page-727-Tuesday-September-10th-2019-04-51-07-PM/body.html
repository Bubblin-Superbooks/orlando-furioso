XXX

"He afterwards the dame for tidings pressed Of those the orc had taken on the shore; And of Lucina above all the rest; If slain or prisoner kept. With kindly lore, She Norandino, in return, addressed; And said Lucina lived, nor need he more Have of her future safety any dread, For the orc on flesh of woman never fed.
