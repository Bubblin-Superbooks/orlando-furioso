LX

"To him the damsel, full of pity, cries: Believe, I covet this no less than thee; But here, surrounded by so many eyes, Is neither time nor opportunity.' — I feel assured' (to her that youth replied) `Were I beloved by you, as you by me, This very night you would find out a place Wherein to solace us some little space.'
