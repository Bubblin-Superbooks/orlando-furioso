LIX

As looked old Aegeus at the accursed board, Seeing it was his son to whom — so willed His wicked consort — that Athenian lord Had given the juice from deadly drugs distilled; Whom he, if he had recognized his sword Though but a little later, would have killed; So looked Marphisa when, disclosed to view, She in the stranger knight Rogero knew;

LX
