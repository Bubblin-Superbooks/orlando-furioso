XCII

That elder is so nimble and so prest, That he seems born to run; he bears away Out of those heaps by lapfulls in his vest The tickets that the different names display. Wherefore and whither he his steps addrest,

Canto say, If you, in sign of pleasure, will attend, With that kind audience ye are wont to lend.
