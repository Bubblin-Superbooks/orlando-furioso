XXI

Within the city gates in frolic sport, Many are seen to ply the festive dance; And here the burghers of the better sort Upon their gay and well-trapt coursers prance. A fairer show remains; the sumptuous court Of barons bold and vassals, who advance, Garnished with what could be procured, of ore And pearl, from Ind and Erythraean shore.

XXII
