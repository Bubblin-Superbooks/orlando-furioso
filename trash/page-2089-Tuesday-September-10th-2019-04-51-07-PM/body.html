LXIX

The mariner subjoined: "Thou saidest well; With gifts so rich he should not her have prest; For, these assaults, these charges, to repel, Not good alike is every human breast. I know not if of wife thou has heard tell (For haply not with us the tale may rest) That in the very sin her husband spied, For which she by his sentence should have died.
