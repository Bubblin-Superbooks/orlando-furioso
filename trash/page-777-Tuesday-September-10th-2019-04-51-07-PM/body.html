X

Rainier and Guido, Richard, Salomon, Ivan, Ughetto, Turpin, and the twain — Angiolin, Angelier — false Ganellon, And Mark and Matthew from St. Michael's plain, With the eight of whom I spake, all set upon The foe, with Edward and Sir Arimane; Who leading succours from the English shore, Had lodged them in the town short time before.
