None knows, observing her with steadfast view, If she of charms or grace have fuller store, Whether her visage most majestic shew, Or beam with genius or with beauty more. "He that would speak — would speak her praises true — (Declares in fine the sculptured marble's lore) The fairest of emprizes would intend, But never bring his noble task to end."
