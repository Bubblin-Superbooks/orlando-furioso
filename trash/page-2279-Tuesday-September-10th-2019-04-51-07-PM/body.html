LV

"Besides that by your edict's tenor none But him can to the damsel lift his eyes, — Is she deserved by deeds of valour done, What other is so worthy of the prize? — Should she by him that loves her best be won, None passes him, nor with the warrior vies; And he is here to fight against all foes That would in arms his right in her oppose."
