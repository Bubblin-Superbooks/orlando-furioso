LVIII

"Damsels and dames withdrawn — with all the rest — Pages and chamberlains, when now we lay, One and the other, in our bed undrest, With kindled torches, counterfeiting day; Marvel not, lady,' (her I thus addrest,)That I return after such short delay; For, haply, thou imagined, that again Thou shouldst not see me until Heaven knows when.
