LI

"And ever will be while this life I bear; Which, albeit 'tis with yours sore laden, still Daily for you is risked with them that are The best of France; and — be he who he will — There is not mortal living, who will dare To say Sobrino's deeds were ever ill: Yea, many who vaunt more, amid your host, Have not so much, nay lighter, cause for boast.
