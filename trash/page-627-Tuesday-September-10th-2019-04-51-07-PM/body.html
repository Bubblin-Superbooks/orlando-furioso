XXVI

"For such desert, Heaven's bounty not alone Designs he should the imperial garland bear, — Augustus', Trajan's, Mark's, Severus', crown; But that of every farthest land should wear, Which here and there extends, as yet unknown, Yielding no passage to the sun and year; And wills that in his time Christ's scattered sheep Should be one flock, beneath one Shepherd's keep.
