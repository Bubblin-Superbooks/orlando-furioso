XI

Adorned with scarlet hat, and scarlet pall, His nephew Benedict, lo! there I see; With him Campeggio and Mantua's cardinal; Glory and light of the consistory; And (if I dote not mark how one and all )

In face and gesture show such mighty glee At my return, no easy task 'twould seem So vast an obligation to redeem.
