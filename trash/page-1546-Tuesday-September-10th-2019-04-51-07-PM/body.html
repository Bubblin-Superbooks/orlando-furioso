XV

When shining arms at distance she perceives, Or any thing that speaks a cavalier, 'Tis her desired Rogero, she believes; And her fair eyes and brows are seen to clear. If footman, or unarmed, the maid conceives, It is a courier from the youthful peer; And, though fallacious every hope she feeds, Another and another aye succeeds.

XVI
