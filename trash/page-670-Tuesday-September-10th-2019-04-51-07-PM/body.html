XI

"When I from Nicosia thee expected (When thou wast journeying to the plenar court) To cheer me, — left with fever sore infected, And in the dread of death, — I heard report That thou wast gone to Syria; and dejected By that ill tiding, suffered in such sort, I, all unable to pursue thy quest, Had nigh with this right hand transfixt my breast.
