They tell Rogero that, as newly wed The combat he in person should refuse. "Take ye no further pains," the warrior said, "For such would be for me a foul excuse." The Tartar's arms were brought, which cut the thread Of more delay and of all further truce: With spurs Orlando deck'd the youthful lord, King Charlemagne begirt him with the sword.
