LI

Gradasso's second offer seemed to be Most opportune to King Troyano's son; And much he blest the chances of the sea, Which him upon that desert isle had thrown: Yet would not upon any pact agree, — Nay, not to repossess Biserta's town — Gradasso should for him in fight contend; Deeming too sore his honour 'twoud offend.
