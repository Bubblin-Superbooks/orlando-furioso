XXXIV

"For one ill-born, a hundred yet behind, Will bear (replied to hazard all content, )

— Slain, burnt alive, to let them to the wind Scatter my ashes, rather than consent. — My people seek to move my stedfast mind, By prayer and by protest, from this intent; And threat to yield my city up and me, Lest all be lost through my obduracy.
