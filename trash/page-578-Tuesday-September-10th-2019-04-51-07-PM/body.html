LVIII

"If a man merits love by loving, I Yours by my love deserve; if it is won By birth, — who boasts a genealogy Like me, the puissant Agricano's son? By riches, — who with me in wealth can vie. That in dominion yield to God alone? By courage, — I to-day (I ween have proved )

That I for courage merit to be loved."
