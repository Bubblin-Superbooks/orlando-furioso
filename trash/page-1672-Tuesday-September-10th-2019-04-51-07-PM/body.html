XLI

"When I perceive that, through the warrior's mean, Extinguished is my father's every foe; And, conquered by himself, that knight is seen — Friendless, through us — I now the masque forego; What I, from him, beneath a flattering mien, Had hitherto concealed, I plainly show; — What deep and deadly hate by bosom fired, And that I but to work his death desired.
