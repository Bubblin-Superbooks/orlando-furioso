LXXXIV

Hence to descend towards the sea or port The way across the place of combat lies; Nor was there other passage, long or short. Sir Guido so to his companions cries: And having ceased his comrades to exhort, To do their best set forth in silent wise, And in the place appeared, amid the throng, Head of a squad above a hundred strong.
