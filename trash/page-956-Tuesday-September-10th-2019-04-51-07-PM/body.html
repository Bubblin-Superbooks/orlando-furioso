LXXXVI

Sir Guido and the cavaliers who go Beneath that champion's guidance, and before The others bold Marphisa, were not slow To strike, and laboured hard to force the door. But such a storm of darts from ready bow, Dealing on all sides death or wounding sore, Was rained in fury on the troop forlorn, They feared at last to encounter skaith and scorn.

LXXXVII
