CXXXI

To warlike Rodomont, with goodly cheer And kindlier mien, the landlord honour paid; For he the port of an illustrious peer In his guest's lofty presence saw pourtrayed. But, sore beside himself, the cavalier Had scarce his heart within him, which had strayed To her — whilere his own — in his despite; Nor word escaped the melancholy knight.
