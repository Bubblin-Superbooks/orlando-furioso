XV

"Amid the hundred cities of old Crete, Was the Dictaean the most rich and bright; Of fair and amorous dames the joyous seat, Joyous with festive sports from morn to night: And (as her townsmen aye were wont to greet The stranger with such hospitable rite )

They welcomed these, it little lacked but they Granted them o'er their households sovereign sway.
