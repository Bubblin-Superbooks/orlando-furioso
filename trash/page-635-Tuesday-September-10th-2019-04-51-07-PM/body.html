XLIII

"Within six miles, no further, shalt thou light (Pursued the hermit on the bloody seat, )

Where dwells a giant, horrible to sight, Exceeding every stature by eight feet. From him wayfaring man or errant knight Would vainly hope with life to make retreat; For some the felon quarters, some he flays, And some he swallows quick, and some he slays.
