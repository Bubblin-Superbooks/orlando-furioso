LXIV

Thence he departed; but he first renewed His compact with Montalban's knight — that so His Agramant convinced of perjury stood — Him and his evil sect he would forego. That day no further feats of hardihood Rogero will perform against the foe: He but demands of all that make for Arles, Who first broke faith, King Agramant or Charles?
