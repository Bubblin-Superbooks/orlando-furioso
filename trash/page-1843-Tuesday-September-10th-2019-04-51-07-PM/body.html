XXI

'Twere long to tell how, with those worthies met, Guido rejoiced to see Marphisa there; Gryphon and Aquilant, and Sansonet, That with her in the cruel city were; Vivian, and Malagigi, and Richardet; Who, when Maganza's traitors made repair, With those ill purchasers of Spain to trade, Found such a faithful comrade in the maid.

XXII
