A suit of arms was prize of the assay, Presented to the king some days before; Which late a merchant found upon the way Returning from Armenia; this the more To grace, a vest, with noblest tissue gay, The Syrian king subjoined, so powdered o'er With jewels, gold, and pearls in rich device, They made the meed a thing of passing price.

LXXXIII

If the good king had known the panoply, This he had held above all others dear; Nor this had given, as full of courtesy, To be contented for with sword and spear. 'Twere long to tell who so unworthily Had erst mistreated thus the goodly gear, That lay the way the harness had been strowed, A prey to whosoever past the road.
