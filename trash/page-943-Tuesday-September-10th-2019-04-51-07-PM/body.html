LVIII

"And him she left with Alexandria, heir To this famed city, which from her was hight, So he and all who his successors were, Should guard the law which willed, whatever wight, Conducted hither by his cruel star, Upon this miserable land did light, Should have his choice to perish by the knife, Or singly with ten foes contend to strife.
