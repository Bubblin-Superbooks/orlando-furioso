LXXIII

His cousin Richardetto, as behoved, Was courteously received by Aldigier; Who him as dearly as a brother loved, And made Rogero for his sake good cheer; But not with wonted welcome; — inly moved — He even wore a visage sad and drear: For he, that day, ill-tidings had received, And hence in heart and face the warrior grieved.
