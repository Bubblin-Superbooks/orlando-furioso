LIII

"The monarch well defends him from the foe, All over bathed with blood of hostile vein. But valour stoops at last to numbers; lo! The king is taken, is conveyed to Spain; And all upon Pescara's lord bestow And him of that inseparable twain — Of Guasto hight — the praise and prime renown For that great king captived and host o'erthrown.
