LXII

Leo will enter not the town; but nigh Pitches his broad pavilions on the plain; And his arrival by an embassy Makes known that day to royal Charlemagne. Well pleased is he; and visits testify And many gifts the monarch's courteous vein. His journey's cause the Grecian prince displayed, And to dispatch his suit the sovereign prayed:
